Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated coffee beverage brewed by forcing hot water under pressure through finely-ground coffee beans. It is the base for many coffee drinks, including lattes, cappuccinos, and macchiatos.

Latte

A latte is a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk. It is typically topped with a small amount of milk foam. Lattes can be flavored with syrups or spices, such as vanilla or cinnamon.

Cappuccino

A cappuccino is a coffee drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It is often topped with cocoa powder or cinnamon. Cappuccinos are known for their rich, creamy texture and strong coffee flavor.

Macchiato

A macchiato is a coffee drink made with espresso and a small amount of steamed milk or milk foam. The name "macchiato" means "stained" or "spotted" in Italian, referring to the way the milk is added to the espresso.
